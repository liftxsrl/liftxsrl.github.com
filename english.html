Automated Vertical and Horizontal Warehouses: Steps Toward Your Warehouse Automation

Our Product

The LiftX Automated Vertical Warehouse, developed by LiftX, is an automated system designed to simplify the organization and management of goods. This innovative warehouse consists of a series of modules where goods are carefully positioned.

Users can easily request a specific module through an intuitive control panel, which provides details about the contents of each module, ensuring quick and efficient access to the desired goods.

Why Choose an Automated Vertical Warehouse?

The Liftx automated warehouse draws inspiration from Lego Mindmaster technology: same flexibility in combining different pieces according to loading and unloading needs; interchangeability of pieces; minimal structure footprint thanks to a limited but ad hoc use of components; reliability guaranteed by the precision with which the components are produced.

How Does Liftx Work?

To interact with the system, the user can rely on a control panel strategically located near the access of the Vertical Warehouse.

At the user's request, the control panel allows the initiation of the "block" movement process. Through a sophisticated transport system, the compartments containing the requested goods are precisely and rapidly moved to the access area.

This is made possible through an intelligent and automated mechanism, involving a machine that moves on a conveyor belt. The latter is designed to accurately select the desired modules and transfer them to the access position, where they can be retrieved or inserted according to the user's needs.
