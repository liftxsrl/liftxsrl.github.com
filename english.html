Our product


The Automatic Vertical Warehouse, fruit of the development of LiftX,
is configured as an automated system designed
to simplify the organization and management of goods.

This innovative warehouse consists of a series of b
where goods are carefully placed.

Users can easily request
a specific module through an intuitive control panel,
which provides details on the content of each module,
thus ensuring quick and efficient access to the desired goods.






Why choose an automatic vertical warehouse?

The LIftx automatic warehouse is inspired by Lego technology
Mindmaster: same flexibility in combining different pieces
depending on loading and unloading requirements; parts interchangeability;
minimum structure space thanks to a limited but ad hoc use of the pieces;
reliability guaranteed by the precision with which the components are produced.







How does Liftx work?


Come funziona Liftx?

To interact with the system, the user can rely on a control panel
strategically located near the entrance to the Vertical Warehouse.

At the user’s request, the control panel allows the process to start
handling of "blocks". Through a sophisticated transport system,
compartments containing the requested goods are moved precisely and
speed to the access area.

This is made possible through an intelligent and automated mechanism,
involving a machine moving on a tape
transporter.

The latter is designed to precisely select
modules desired and transfer them to the access location, where they can be fetched
or inserted according to the user’s needs.
